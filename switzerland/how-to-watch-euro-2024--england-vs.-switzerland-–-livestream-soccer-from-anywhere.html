Pretournament favorites England get another chance to prove they're worthy of the billing as they take on Switzerland on Saturday in this UEFA Euro 2024 quarterfinal. 

Despite reaching the last eight, England continue to underwhelm in Germany. Gareth's Southgate's team were seconds away from crashing out of the tournament against unfancied Slovakia before Jude Bellingham's dramatic 95th minute overhead kick. They face an in-form Switzerland side that will fancy their chances of progressing further after dumping out defending champions Italy with an impressive 2-0 win in Berlin last Saturday.

England play Switzerland on Saturday, July 6, at the Düsseldorf Arena. Kickoff is set for 6 p.m. CET local time in Germany, making it a 5 p.m. BST start in the UK. In the US and Canada, it's a 12 p.m. ET or 9 a.m. PT start, while the game gets underway at 2 a.m. AEST in Australia on Sunday.

How to watch England vs. Switzerland in the US without cable
