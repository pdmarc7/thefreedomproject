Ivanka Trump reenters the political scene by joining her father, Donald Trump, on stage following his speech at the RNC

19 July, 2024

Ivanka Trump made a return to the political scene and her father's campaign trail on Thursday night, joining her family on stage to conclude the Republican National Convention.

Ivanka Trump, who served as a senior adviser during her father's administration, had distanced herself from politics after his departure from the White House in January 2021, following the January 6 Capitol riot.

After leaving Washington, DC, Ivanka and her spouse Jared Kushner relocated to Miami with their three children.

When Donald Trump announced his candidacy for a third term in November 2022, Ivanka stated her intention to remain out of the political arena, prioritizing her family life. She expressed her love for her father but chose to focus on her young children and private life.

On Thursday night, Ivanka made a rare public appearance at the RNC as her father accepted the Republican nomination and delivered a lengthy 93-minute speech, marking his first public remarks since a recent assassination attempt.

Wearing a white suit and accompanied by Kushner and other Trump family members in the family box, Ivanka smiled as Melania Trump received a warm welcome from the audience in the Milwaukee auditorium.

Both Ivanka and Melania, who have largely stayed out of Trump's 2024 campaign, joined him on stage as he and vice presidential candidate JD Vance officially accepted the GOP nomination.

The event continued with several Trump family members joining the stage after Trump's speech, as the crowd cheered and red, white, and blue balloons filled the venue.

During his speech, Trump expressed gratitude to his family, highlighting their support and contributions. He praised Melania for her letter calling for national unity and thanked his children and grandchildren.

Despite the show of unity, Ivanka and Melania did not participate in the first three days of the convention, unlike Don Jr., Eric, and Tiffany, who were present throughout. Neither Ivanka nor Melania gave speeches at the event, unlike their participation in previous conventions.

Even though Ivanka has kept a low profile in her father's 2024 campaign, she issued a statement following the recent assassination attempt on him. She expressed appreciation for the support and the swift actions of law enforcement, and conveyed her ongoing prayers for the country and her father.
