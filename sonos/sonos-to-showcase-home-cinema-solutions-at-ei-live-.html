Sonos to Showcase Home Cinema Solutions at EI Live! 2024

23 July, 2024

Sonos is back for EI Live! 2024 with its biggest Stand to date. Visitors to Stand 93 will see the full line-up of Sonos solutions for home cinema, media rooms and multi-room audio installations. Sonos also returns as headline sponsor for The Smart Building Awards, which takes place alongside the show at the Farnborough Exhibition Centre on Wednesday 19 September.

Installers will have the opportunity to hear and see the latest home cinema solutions from Sonos, including a 7.1 set-up with a Sonos Arc soundbar, Sonos Era 300 rear-speakers and Sonos Sub. Paired with the Sonos Ace headphones – new for 2024 – installers will appreciate how this powerful set-up can now deliver a personalised surround sound experience for individual listeners.

As well as the home cinema experience area, the Sonos stand will showcase the full range of products for professional installs from multi-room audio to media room packages and outdoor installations – including Sonos Amp, Sonos Port, Sonos Outdoor Speakers and the Sonos architectural range of speakers.

There is a dedicated meeting area available on the stand for installers to find out more about Sonos for Professionals and a team of experts will be on hand to demo products and answer questions throughout the event. Stephen Rhead, Sonos Professional Manager for the UK and Ireland, will also be available to discuss the business benefits of becoming a certified Sonos installer.

Stephen says, “We’re excited to be returning to EI Live! this year with our comprehensive portfolio of audio innovations and support for professional installations. We’ll be demonstrating a new approach to home cinema and media room projects, providing installers with more sell-in opportunities and clients with more listening freedom and flexibility. There will be new products on show, as well as a preview of upcoming innovations – so make sure you stop by the Sonos booth and talk to us about what’s new!”

You can also catch Stephen discussing ‘What’s Next for Media Room Audio?” in the Technical Theatre on Wednesday 19 September at 14:50-15:25. This ‘fireside’ discussion will explore the new demands of audio in the home, how clients are consuming TV, gaming and music audio and why the professional industry must adapt to stay ahead of the trend.

Sonos says it is committed to empowering professionals and enabling them to deliver exceptional audio solutions. Visit Sonos at EI Live! On Stand 93, to find out more.
