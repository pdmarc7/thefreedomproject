2024 MLS All-Star Game updated roster released; Messi listed as unavailable

22 July, 2024

COLUMBUS, Ohio — Major League Soccer has announced which players are unavailable for the 2024 MLS All-Star Game in Columbus on Wednesday.

The match is being held at Lower.com Field on July 24. Five Crew players and Inter Miami star Lionel Messi made the roster, though MLS announced Monday that Messi will be unavailable. He suffered a ligament injury to his right ankle during the Copa América final last weekend.

Other unavailable MLS All-Stars include Miles Robinson, Luis Suárez, Cristian "Chicho" Arango and Ryan Gauld.

It’s unclear if the players listed as unavailable will still attend the game in Columbus.

Eighteen clubs are represented on this year’s All-Star Game roster: FC Cincinnati, Colorado Rapids, Columbus Crew, D.C. United, FC Dallas, Houston Dynamo FC, Inter Miami CF, LA Galaxy, LAFC, Minnesota United FC, CF Montréal, Nashville SC, New York City FC, Portland Timbers, Real Salt Lake, St. Louis CITY SC, Toronto FC, and Vancouver Whitecaps FC.
