USA Rugby Will Be Favorites Against Romania

Yes, Romania played in the 2023 Rugby World Cup. No, Romania didn’t actually qualify for the Rugby World Cup. 

It’s complicated. And it showed. 

Romania played in the 2023 Rugby World Cup because Spain was disqualified for playing ineligible players. The national team won’t apologize for playing in the Rugby World Cup, but they did lose three games to current No. 1 and No. 2 South Africa and Ireland, who play each other on July 6, and Scotland by a combined score of 242-8. 

That’s not a typo. 

But USA didn't qualify for the Rugby World Cup after a massive match against Portugal in November 2022 ended in a 16-16 draw. That eliminated them from a tournament they had played in six straight times. 

So despite playing a national team that did play in France in 2023, Bendon said the US is the heavy favorite against Romania. 

“They have a very good chance of winning that game,” Bendon said. “Head-to-head, the USA are a better team than Romania in terms of the talent they have available.”

USA Rugby Kits For Romania Match

USA Rugby vs. Romania History

The matchup is part of the America Series and continues the rivalry between the US and Romania. This is the 100th anniversary of the USA vs. Romania match at the 1924 Paris Olympic Games and comes just one day after the Fourth of July. 

The United States won the gold medal in 1924 in large part to their win over eventual bronze medalist Romania. The Eagles were heavy underdogs in the match against Romania, but Charles Doe scored 13 points and the Americans won 37-0 before beating France 17-3 in the gold medal game.
