Heinous Warhammer 40K: Space Marine 2 Statue a Guaranteed Conversation Starter

23 July, 2024

We're about as excited for the upcoming Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine 2 as it is politely acceptable to be and are well aware of the grimdark nature of the universe in which it takes place. Still, the statue offered alongside the game's pricey Collector's Edition is too rich for our blood, a grisly eyesore in the vein of that legendarily disgusting Dead Island: Riptide bust.

We hope that whoever this 8.2" x 6.9" hand-painted resin statue of Lieutenant Titus wading through Tyranid ichor is for is pleased with their purchase, and we salute their dedication. Available exclusively through publisher Focus Entertainment's store, the $250 Collector's Edition can be pre-ordered now, and in addition to Titus doing what he does best, you get a 172-page art book, the Macragge's Chosen DLC, the Ultramaines Champion pack, the game's Season Pass, SteelBook case, and four days early access.
