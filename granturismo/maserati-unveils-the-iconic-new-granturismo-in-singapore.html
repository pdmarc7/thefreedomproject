Maserati Unveils the Iconic New GranTurismo in Singapore

23 July, 2024

A new era of performance and luxury.

The highly-anticipated Maserati GranTurismo has made its grand debut in Singapore, ushering in a new chapter in Maserati's illustrious 75-year history. This new iteration of the GranTurismo combines the high performance of a sports car with the comfort required for long-distance travel. Available in both a powerful internal combustion engine version (Modena) and an innovative 100% electric version (Folgore), the GranTurismo sets a new standard in the automotive world.

The new Maserati GranTurismo is designed to offer exceptional performance, advanced technology, and unparalleled comfort and elegance. It stands out for its sporty design and the distinctive Maserati sound, embodying the concept of “The Others Just Travel.” Whether you opt for the traditional power of the Modena or the cutting-edge electric power of the Folgore, the GranTurismo promises an exhilarating driving experience.

GranTurismo Modena: Available Now

The GranTurismo Modena is now available for order in Singapore, priced at SGD 688,800 (excluding COE). This version comes with a five-year unlimited mileage warranty and five years of servicing, ensuring peace of mind for new owners. The Modena model is perfect for those who desire a high-performance sports car with the comfort needed for long journeys.

GranTurismo Folgore: Coming Soon

For those looking forward to a fully electric experience, the GranTurismo Folgore will arrive in Singapore in early 2025. This innovative model represents Maserati’s commitment to sustainability without compromising on performance or luxury.

Public Viewing Events:

To celebrate the arrival of the Maserati GranTurismo Modena in Singapore, the car will be available for public viewing at several car displays across the city. Car enthusiasts and potential buyers can get an up-close look at this iconic vehicle and appreciate its design and features firsthand.

Date: 22–28 July 2024

Location: i12 Katong, Main Atrium, 112 E Coast Road, Singapore 

Date: 16–25 August 2024
