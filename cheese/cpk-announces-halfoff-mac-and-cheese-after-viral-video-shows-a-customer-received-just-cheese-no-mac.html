CPK Announces Half-Off Mac and Cheese After Viral Video Shows a Customer Received 'Just Cheese, No Mac'

22 July, 2024

A cheesy takeout mistake resulted in a deal for all California Pizza Kitchen customers.

The pizza chain is offering half-off mac and cheese for the rest of July after a customer's mac and cheese order arrived with all cheese and no macaroni. TikToker @fumptruck posted the macaroni mishap on July 17. In the video, which has over 3.3 million views, the user posted that she was having “the worst week of my life” and a takeout mixup was the cherry on top.

“I’m injured, my boyfriend and I broke up so I ordered mac and cheese from California Pizza Kitchen,” she said in the clip. “And they just sent me cheese. Just cheese, no mac. There’s no noodles.”

She panned the camera to the takeout bag to prove that the noodles were not boxed separately.

“Are you f---ing kidding me? I’m gonna cry,” she laughed in the video, adding that when she called the restaurant, they told her that she must have “ordered wrong.” 

In the days following the viral TikTok, California Pizza Kitchen sent the user a voucher for a year of free mac and cheese and a huge box of noodles. A few days later, on July 20, the pizza joint responded with their own TikTok.

CPK’s Senior Director of Culinary Development Paul Pszybylski said that he heard “there’s been a little bit of confusion on how we properly make our mac and cheese” and that he wanted to explain the steps.

“Step one: cook the cheese,” he said matter-of-factly, adding grated cheese to a pan.

Pszybylski continued, “Step two: cook the mac.”

“Step three: add the mac to the cheese,” he said, raising his eyebrows.

At the end of the clip he explained the nationwide deal they are offering through the end of the month.

“Since we recently we gave one of our guests only half a mac and cheese. We’re going to give all of our guests 50% off mac and cheese,” he said.

To score the deal, customers need to plug in the promo code “cheeseANDmac” at checkout to get half-priced pasta. The deal is available until July 31.

Hungry for more? Fortunately, there are plenty of fast food freebies going on.

McDonald’s is handing out free fries every Friday through the rest of the year. The only requirement is that customers have to make a purchase of $1 or more via the McDonald’s app to get the deal.
