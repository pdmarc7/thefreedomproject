Washington mobilizes resources for Big Horn Fire; Evacuation orders in effect in Klickitat Co.

23 July, 2024

KLICKITAT COUNTY Wash. (KPTV) - The Washington State Fire Marshal has mobilized assistance to help contain the Big Horn Fire, located near the town of Bickleton, their office announced Monday night.

The fire started about 1 p.m., and currently covers about 1,200 acres. Authorities say it is still growing.

The following evacuation orders are in effect:

  • Level 3: Go Now! in the Bickleton area, from Pine Creek east to Hale Road and south to Whitmore Road, including East Road. Note that East Road is closed from the town of Bickleton south to Hale Road.
  • Level 3: Go Now! south of Six Prong to the Columbia River to east of Six Prong Extension and West to East Road/Roosevelt Grade Road.
  • Level 2: Get Set east of Six Prong Extension to Sand Ridge Road, north to Hale Road and south to the Columbia River.
  • Level 2: Get Set from Hale Road south to Six Prong and east to Six Prong Extension.
  • Level 2: Get Set for the Alder Creek Canyon
  • Level 1: Get Ready: Sand Ridge Road to the east to Klickitat County line and South to the Columbia River.
