Apparent suicide at Benjamin L. Hooks Library prompts weeklong closure

23 July, 2024

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (WMC) - The Benjamin L. Hooks Central Library will be closed through July 29 after an apparent suicide inside the building Monday morning.

All other Memphis Public Library branches will remain open.

A source with Memphis Public Libraries says that a man fell from the third-story balcony overlooking the lobby.

The Memphis Police Department confirmed that their investigation indicates it was a suicide.

"This is a heartbreaking and tragic incident that speaks to the importance of mental health care in our community,” Memphis Public Libraries posted to Facebook. “The Benjamin L. Hooks Library will remain closed until Monday, July 29, so our library staff and community can process and have time to access mental health and wellness resources, including grief counselors.”

The Benjamin L. Hooks Central Library is located at 3030 Poplar Avenue in East Memphis.

If you are in need of mental health assistance, help is available 24/7 through the Shelby County HelpLine at 211 or the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline at 988.
