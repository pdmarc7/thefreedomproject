$1M Powerball ticket among lottery wins in Phoenix area; here’s where the tickets were sold

22 July, 2024

PHOENIX (AZFamily) — The Arizona Lottery announced four major lottery wins in drawings this Saturday, including a big Powerball win in Phoenix.

Lottery officials say a $1 million winning Powerball ticket was purchased at a Circle K off 7th Avenue and Roosevelt Street in Phoenix on Saturday.

Another Powerball winner earned $50,000 after shopping Walmart at 43rd Avenue and Union Hills Drive in Glendale.

Saturday’s numbers were 18, 25, 31, 40, 57 and Powerball 04.

They weren’t the only Valley-area lottery winners. A Fantasy 5 Jackpot winner earned $144,002 from their ticket purchased at a Circle K near US 60 and Tomahawk Road in Apache Junction.

Outside the Valley, a big winner in Tucson hit the Triple Twist Jackpot and is now $202,000 richer after a purchase at a Safeway on Golf Links and Wilmot Roads. It marks the third time this jackpot has been hit in one week.
