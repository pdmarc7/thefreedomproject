Beyond the Diamond with the Fountain Lake Cobras’ Caden George

23 July, 2024

FOUNTAIN LAKE -- Fountain Lake High School graduate Caden George made his return to the baseball diamond for his senior campaign this past spring at first base.

Cobras' head coach Justin McArthur said George really developed as a first baseman as a senior and also helped in the outfield.

"He shows up every day with a positive attitude," McArthur said.

George met with The Sentinel-Record to give people a glimpse into what he is like beyond the diamond. The right-handed hitter also played right field. His walkout music was "Can't Tell Me Nothing," by Ye.

What do you like most about playing baseball?

Having all of my best friends on the team.

What is your most memorable moment playing baseball, even going back to when you were a kid?

Making the state tournament my sophomore year.

Do you have any pregame rituals or superstitions?

Eye black

Who is your all-time favorite athlete in any sport?

Anthony Rizzo

Do you have any hobbies or interests outside of sports?

Hunting and fishing

Are you a morning person or a night owl?

Night owl

If you could hang out with any famous person, past or present, for a day, who would that be?

Tom Brady

If you could visit any place in the world, where would you go?

Italy

What is your favorite restaurant and favorite meal?

Village Hibachi, their tuna.

What one person is your biggest inspiration?

My dad.

What is the last book you read or are currently reading?

Last book I read was in English class, that was "Nectar in a Sieve."

What is the best piece of advice you have ever received?

That it always gets better.

What is the last television series that you binge-watched, or are currently watching?

"Breaking Bad"

What is your all-time favorite movie?

"The Waterboy"

Who are some of your favorite social media influencers, or favorite accounts that you follow?

I follow a lot of UFC accounts.

What band or musical artist have you been listening to lately?

Treaty Oak Revival

What is your biggest pet peeve?

When people say something like, 'Keep your word.'

What is your favorite class in school?

Civics and econ.

What are your plans after graduation and what type of career do you plan to pursue?

I am going to go to a trade school and be an electrician.
